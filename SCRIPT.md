## Demo Script

Hi, I'm Anuj, and I built **Smriti**.

Every family manages health the same broken way—WhatsApp messages, paper prescriptions, and half-remembered doctor visits.

Ask any caregiver, *"What did the doctor say last time?"* Nobody remembers exactly.

Ask any chronic patient, *"Did this symptom get worse after that medicine?"* Nobody is tracking that either.

Health apps already exist, but they expect people to manually fill forms and maintain records. Nobody keeps that up.

And health data is the most personal data we own, so people need a memory they can actually trust.

Supermemory solved the memory part.

So I built Smriti on top of it.

Instead of filling forms, you just talk naturally, and your family's health becomes searchable memory.

---

Let's see it in action.

I'll switch to **Care Mode**.

**"Papa ko kal raat neend nahi aayi."**

That's it.

Smriti transcribes what I said, understands the context, structures it into a health event, timestamps it, and prepares it for memory.

Nothing is saved automatically.

I review every memory before it's stored, so the user always stays in control.

---

Voice isn't always convenient.

The exact same intelligence also works through text.

I'll quickly type another health update.

Again, it understands the information and prepares it as structured memory.

---

Now let's upload a prescription.

Most medical history already exists on paper.

Smriti reads the prescription, extracts every medicine and every instruction, and converts them into structured memories.

With one confirmation, the paper becomes searchable memory.

---

Now comes the real value.

**What did the doctor say about salt?**

Instead of searching through chats or prescriptions, Smriti instantly finds the answer with the source.

Now let's ask something harder.

**Has Papa's sleep gotten worse since starting the new blood pressure medicine?**

Smriti connects conversations, prescriptions, symptoms, and timelines across weeks or months to answer questions that people simply can't answer from memory.

---

Before every doctor visit, Smriti can generate a complete visit summary.

Instead of trying to remember everything that happened over the last few months, you walk into the appointment with a complete history.

---

This is running on Supermemory Cloud today.

But I also built and tested the exact same system completely offline—with local models and local memory.

You can deploy it in the cloud for convenience or locally for complete privacy.

Smriti means **memory** in Hindi.

Because your family's health deserves to be remembered.
